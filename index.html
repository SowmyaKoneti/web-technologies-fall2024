Badminton

Intro

Badminton is a popular racquet sport played worldwide. It can be played as a singles or doubles game and is known for its fast-paced action and agility. The objective is to score points by hitting a shuttlecock over a net into the opponent's court.

Quote

"Life's a game of badminton you'll face volleys, hits, and misses. Stay agile, keep swinging" - Robyn Holthouse

Benefits of Playing Badminton

Playing badminton has numerous benefits

  • Improves cardiovascular health
  • Enhances flexibility and agility
  • Builds muscle strength
